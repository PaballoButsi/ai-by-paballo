SmartWork AI is a web-based productivity assistant designed to help office professionals work smarter every day. 
It uses Google's Gemini AI to automate three common workplace challenges — chatting for quick answers, planning your day, and researching any topic instantly.

This app was designed and built using Lovable.ai — an AI-powered app builder — demonstrating the ability to leverage modern AI tools to create real, functional software without traditional coding.
✨ Features

💬 AI Chat Assistant


Ask anything about work, focus, or productivity
Get instant responses powered by Gemini AI
Conversation history saved in the sidebar
Suggested quick-start prompts


📅 AI Task Planner & Scheduler


Paste your task list and AI organizes your day
Prioritizes tasks using the Eisenhower Matrix
Generates a realistic hourly schedule with buffer breaks
Includes a personalized productivity tip
Export or copy your schedule
🔍 AI Research Assistant


Summarize any article, report, or topic instantly
Extracts key insights and recommendations
Simplifies complex information for quick understanding
